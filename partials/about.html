About Me

LET YOUR VIDEO TELL A STORY

I fell in love with video for many reasons; for its creativity, its emotion, but most importantly, for the endless different stories it can tell. Whether it be a passion project, a short film, a product advertisement, or an event that you always want to remember, your idea can become a video you can share with family, friends, and the world.

Video has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember, leading me from home videos to high school projects to a degree in video production. Now, I have the amazing opportunity to create videos for clients across the US.
